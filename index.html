About Me

Hi there, my name is Josh and I am a UI/UX Designer based in Adelaide, South Australia.

I have recently undertaken a big career change from a background in warehousing and logistics and have entered the world of UI/UX Design by completing the UI/UX Design Bootcamp with Adelaide University.

I design clean, minimalistic interfaces that make for delightful user experiences and take a lot of pride in creating ambitious colour schemes that pair well with my new found love of typography.

In my spare time, I’ll typically be enjoying a favourite movie (likely a Tarantino), entertaining friends and family in my newly landscaped backyard, or going bushwalking with my fiancé.
